# Postoperative Form

The postoperative form documents the patient's recovery from anaesthesia in the recovery room (PACU).

## Modified Aldrete Score

The Aldrete score assesses readiness for discharge from the recovery room. Score each criterion from 0 to 2:

| Criterion | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|-----------|---|---|---|
| **Activity** | No movement | 2 extremities | All extremities |
| **Respiration** | Apnoeic | Shallow/limited | Deep breath & cough |
| **Circulation** | BP ±50% of pre-op | BP ±20–49% of pre-op | BP ±20% of pre-op |
| **Consciousness** | No response | Arousable | Fully awake |
| **SpO₂** | &lt;90% with O₂ | &gt;90% with O₂ | &gt;92% on room air |

**Total ≥ 9** indicates readiness for discharge from the recovery room.

The total is calculated automatically and displayed with a colour indicator:
- 🟢 ≥ 9 — Ready
- 🟡 7–8 — Monitor
- 🔴 < 7 — Continue recovery

## Recovery vitals

- **Temperature** (°C)
- **Pain NRS** — Numeric Rating Scale 0–10 (slider)
- **PONV** — Postoperative nausea and vomiting (Yes/No)
- **Time in PACU** — minutes spent in the recovery room

## Postoperative state at discharge (disposition)

Select where the patient is discharged to:

| Disposition | Colour |
|-------------|--------|
| **Ward** | Green |
| **PACU** (extended stay) | Amber |
| **ICU** | Red |

Add clinical notes for the receiving team in the free text field below.

## Handover instructions

For patients going to the **ward** or **PACU**, a checklist of standard handover instructions is available. Tick all that apply:

- Pain management plan
- Nausea/vomiting management
- Fluid balance monitoring
- Wound care instructions
- Mobility restrictions
- DVT prophylaxis
- Special monitoring requirements
- Specific drug instructions
- Follow-up plan

These instructions appear in **full text** on the printed protocol — not abbreviated codes.

## Completing the case

Click **Complete case** to finalise all three forms and generate the protocol. The case status changes to **Case finished** on the dashboard.

:::warning Cannot undo
Once a case is marked complete, editing requires reopening it. Always verify all data before completing.
:::
