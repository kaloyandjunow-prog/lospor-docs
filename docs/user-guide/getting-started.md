# Getting Started

## Creating an account

1. Go to **[app.lospor.org](https://app.lospor.org)**
2. Click **Register** on the login page
3. Fill in your details:
   - First and last name
   - Professional title (e.g. Dr., Assoc. Prof.)
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - **Institution** — select your hospital from the list (Bulgarian NHIF institutions are included)
4. Click **Create account**

You will be logged in immediately with the **Member** role.

:::info Institution not in the list?
If your institution is not listed, contact your system administrator. The institution list is based on the Bulgarian NHIF register.
:::

## Logging in

Visit **[app.lospor.org](https://app.lospor.org)** and enter your email and password.

## First steps

After logging in you will see the **Dashboard**. From here you can:

- View your recorded cases
- Start a new case using the **New case** button in the top navigation
- Access ongoing cases via the **Ongoing cases** button
- Open settings via the **⚙** gear icon

## Guided tour

Click the **?** button in the top-right corner of any page to launch the guided tour for that section. On the dashboard, the tour explains all the main features. When filling in a form, the tour explains each section.

To see a realistic example of a completed case, click **?** → **Example case walkthrough** — this opens a pre-filled colectomy case and guides you through all four forms.

## Language

LOSPOR is available in **English** and **Bulgarian**. To change the language:

1. Click the **⚙** settings icon
2. Under **UI Options**, find **Language**
3. Select **English** or **Български**

The change takes effect immediately and is remembered for future sessions.
