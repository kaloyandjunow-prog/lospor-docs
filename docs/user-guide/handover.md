# Case Handover

LOSPOR allows you to transfer a case to a colleague within the same institution — for example, when a shift changes and a different anaesthesiologist takes over a case.

## Sending a handover

1. On the **dashboard**, find the case you want to hand over
2. Click the **Hand over** button (person icon) on the right side of the case row
3. A dropdown appears listing your colleagues at the same institution
4. Search for the colleague's name and select them
5. Click **Send request**

The case row will show an **Awaiting acceptance** badge while the request is pending.

:::info Members must accept
When a regular member (non-HOD) sends a handover, the recipient must **accept** it before the case transfers. The recipient sees a notification on their dashboard.
:::

## Receiving a handover

When a colleague sends you a case, a yellow **Pending handovers** banner appears at the top of your dashboard.

Each pending handover shows:
- The procedure name and diagnosis
- The name of the colleague sending it
- The case code

You can **Accept** or **Decline** each one:
- **Accept** — the case transfers to your account immediately. It disappears from the sender's dashboard and appears in yours.
- **Decline** — the request is cancelled. The case stays with the original owner.

## Head of Department assignment

Heads of department can **assign** cases to any member of the institution instantly, without requiring the recipient's acceptance.

1. On the dashboard, click **Hand over / Assign** on any case visible in the institution list
2. Select the target colleague
3. Click **Assign now**

The case transfers immediately.

## After transfer

Once a case is transferred:
- The **original owner loses access** to the case
- The **new owner** can continue filling in the forms or view the completed case
- The transfer is logged internally for audit purposes

The case can be further handed over to another colleague by the new owner.
